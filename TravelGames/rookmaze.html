How to Play: Knight's Maze

Knight's Maze is a strategic battle where players move like chess knights in L-shapes across a board.

Setup

  • Player X starts in the top-left corner
  • Player O starts in the bottom-right corner
  • Choose your board size (6x6, 8x8, or 10x10)
  • Choose to play against a friend or AI

Movement

  • Move like a chess knight: 2 squares in one direction, then 1 square perpendicular (L-shape)
  • Click any square to attempt a move
  • Invalid moves will flash orange
  • Previously occupied squares become blocked (dark green)

Winning

  • Win by capturing your opponent (moving to their square)
  • Win by blocking all of your opponent's possible moves

AI Mode

  • Play against an AI opponent that makes random valid moves
  • Toggle AI mode in the menu before starting
