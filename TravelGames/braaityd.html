BraaiTyd Pro Guide

How to use:

  • Select your meat type from the categories
  • Press Start Cooking to begin
  • Timer cycles through: Braai → Turn → Braai → Rest
  • Voice guidance announces each stage change
  • Adjust time with +/- 30s buttons
  • Screen stays on during cooking
  • Toggle between English/Afrikaans with the language button
  • Different meats have optimized cooking times
  • Get cooking tips during each stage

Perfect for camping, picnics, or backyard braais!
