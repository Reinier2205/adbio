How to Play Yatzy

Upper Section — Score the sum of the matching dice (Ones through Sixes). Reach 63 pts for a +35 bonus.

3 of a Kind — Three matching dice → sum of all dice.

4 of a Kind — Four matching dice → sum of all dice.

Full House — Three of one + two of another → 25 pts.

Small Straight — 4 sequential dice → 30 pts.

Large Straight — 5 sequential dice → 40 pts.

Yatzy — All five dice match → 50 pts.

Chance — Any dice → sum of all dice.

Tap any score cell to enter or edit a score. Tap Cancel to leave it blank (zero).
