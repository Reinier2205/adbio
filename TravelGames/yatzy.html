How to Play Yatzy

Turns

Players take turns. On your turn, tap any highlighted cell in your column to enter a score. The turn passes automatically once you score.

Upper Section (Ones – Sixes)

Score the sum of matching dice only. E.g. three 4s = 12. Reach 63 pts in the upper section to earn a +35 bonus.

Lower Section

3 of a Kind — At least three matching dice → score the sum of all 5 dice (min 5, max 30).

4 of a Kind — At least four matching dice → score the sum of all 5 dice (min 5, max 30).

Full House — Three of one + two of another → 25 pts. Tap to toggle on/off.

Small Straight — Any 4 sequential dice (e.g. 2-3-4-5) → 30 pts. Tap to toggle.

Large Straight — All 5 sequential dice (1-2-3-4-5 or 2-3-4-5-6) → 40 pts. Tap to toggle.

Yatzy — All five dice the same → 50 pts. Tap to toggle.

Chance — Any dice → score the sum of all 5 dice (min 5, max 30).

Scoring Tips

You must score every category exactly once. If you can't make a combination, tap the cell and choose Scratch (0) to forfeit that category.

Any entered score can be corrected at any time by tapping it again.
