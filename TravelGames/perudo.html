The Art of the Pirate's Bluff

The Objective

Listen close, ye scoundrel. Your greatest weapon isn't your diceâ€”it's your poker face. Be the last pirate standing with at least one die in your cup. When the "Dudo" (Liar) is called, someone is losing a die. Make sure it isn't you, or you'll be walking the plank.

The Pirate's Code

Every pirate makes a collective bid on how many dice of a certain value exist under all cups combined.

  • The Bid: "I think there be Seven 5s on this table."
  • The Response: The next pirate must either Raise the bid or call Dudo (Liar!).
  • Aces are Wild: 1s count as whatever number is being bid! They be the ultimate game-changers.

The Bidding Ladder

Secret Strategy Tips

Master these hidden tactics to dominate the tavern:

The Rule of Thirds

Statistically, 1/3 of the dice on the table will match your bid (Value + Aces). Use this math to spot a lying dog.

The "Phantom" Bid

Bid high on a number you don't have. It traps others into thinking those dice are safe on the table.

The Ace Trap

Switching to Aces resets the pressure. Use it to flush out the other pirates' hidden wild cards.

The Palafico Round

When you have 1 die left, you are Palafico. In this special round, Aces are NOT wild. Don't forget the change in the code!
