The Objective

The Pirate's Code

Every pirate makes a collective bid on how many dice of a certain value exist under all cups combined.

  • The Bid: "I think there be Seven 5s on this table."
  • The Response: The next pirate must either Raise the bid or call Dudo (Liar!).
  • Aces are Wild: 1s count as whatever number is being bid! They be the ultimate game-changers on the high seas.

Secret Strategy Tips

Master these hidden tactics to dominate the tavern:

The Rule of Thirds

Statistically, 1/3 of the dice on the table will match your bid (Value + Aces). Use this math to spot a lying dog.

The "Phantom" Bid

Bid high on a number you don't have. If you have zero 6s, bid "Four 6s." It traps others into thinking the 6s are safe.

The Ace Trap

Switching to Aces early confuses the crew. If you have two 1s, jump to them quickly to flush out the other pirates' hidden Aces.

The Palafico Round

When you have 1 die left, you are Palafico. In this special round, Aces are NOT wild. Don't let your guard down, matey!

Play at your own risk. May the best liar win.
