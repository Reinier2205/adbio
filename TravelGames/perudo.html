⚓ PERUDO ⚓

The Pirate's Code of Bluff & Dice

The Objective

Listen, ye salty dog! Bluff, lie, and call out the scoundrels. Be the last pirate with at least one die in your cup. When "Dudo!" (liar) echoes across the table, someone loses a die and walks the plank. The sea claims the meek — only the bold survive.

The Pirate's Code

Every turn, a pirate makes a bid on how many dice of a certain face value lie under ALL cups combined.

  • The Bid: "Seven 5s" means you claim there are at least seven dice showing 5 (including Aces as wilds).
  • Response: Next pirate must RAISE the bid (count or value) or shout DUDO (LIAR!)
  • Aces are WILD: 1s count as any number being bid — they shift the tides of battle!

Secret Strategy Tips

Master these tavern-hidden tactics to sink your rivals:

The Rule of Thirds

Statistically, ~1/3 of total dice on the table will match your bid (value + wild aces). Use this math to detect a lying dog before they send you to Davey Jones.

The "Phantom" Bid

Bid high on a number you don't possess. It makes others believe those dice are safe. They'll overcommit — then you strike with Dudo!

The Ace Trap

Switching to Aces halves quantity — a great way to reset pressure. Use it to flush out opponents' hidden wild cards, then leave aces to double & add one.

Palafico Awareness

When any sailor has only 1 die, Aces are NOT wild. That changes everything! In that round, bid with precision — wildcard advantage vanishes.

PIRATE'S CODE

🎲 HOW TO PLAY

Goal: Be last survivor with at least 1 die. Call "Dudo!" when the bid is impossible.

⚓ BIDDING RULES

  • RAISE COUNT: Increase quantity, same face. (ex: Six 4s)
  • RAISE VALUE: Increase face value, same quantity. (ex: Five 6s)
  • JUMP TO ACES: Halve quantity (round UP) → move to Aces (1s).
  • LEAVE ACES: Double Ace bid, add 1 → any normal value (2-6).

💀 DUDO (Liar!)

If you think the bid is too high, shout Dudo! All dice revealed. If total dice (including wild aces) meet or exceed bid → accuser loses a die. Otherwise, the bidder loses a die.

✨ ACE RULE & PALAFICO

Normally Aces are wild and count for every number. BUT in Palafico (when a player has exactly 1 die left), Aces are no longer wild! Strategy changes.

Pro tip: Master the "Ladder" transitions to control the table!
