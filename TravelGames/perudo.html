⚓ PERUDO ⚓

The Pirate's Bluff & Dice

The Objective

Be the last pirate standing with at least one die. Call "Dudo!" (liar) when you suspect the bid is impossible. Lose a die, walk the plank. Bluff or be sunk.

The Pirate's Code

Each bid states how many dice of a certain value are under all cups combined.

  • The Bid: "Seven 5s" → at least seven dice showing 5 (Aces are wild and count as 5s).
  • Raise by increasing quantity or value, or jump to/from Aces.
  • Aces are WILD: 1s count for the bid number!

Secret Strategy Tips

Master these hidden tactics to dominate the tavern:

The Rule of Thirds

Statistically, ~1/3 of total dice match your bid (value + wild aces). Use math to catch liars.

The "Phantom" Bid

Bid high on a number you don't have. Others assume those dice are present — then call Dudo!

The Ace Trap

Jumping to Aces halves the quantity, easing pressure. Then "Leave Aces" to double+add-one — a powerful swing.

Palafico Awareness

When any pirate has 1 die, Aces are no longer wild. Adjust your bids instantly — no free wildcards.

PIRATE'S CODE

🎲 How to Play

Goal: Be the last pirate with at least one die. Shout "Dudo!" when you think the bid is too high.

⚓ Raising the Bid

  • Raise Count: Same face, higher quantity (e.g., Six 4s)
  • Raise Value: Same quantity, higher face (e.g., Five 6s)
  • Jump to Aces: Halve quantity (round up) → bid Aces
  • Leave Aces: Double Ace quantity +1 → any normal value (2–6)

💀 Dudo (Liar!)

Reveal all dice. If total dice (including wild Aces) meet or exceed bid → accuser loses a die. Otherwise, bidder loses.

✨ Aces & Palafico

Normally Aces are wild. Palafico (any player has 1 die): Aces are NOT wild. Strategy changes!
