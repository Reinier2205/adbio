Pathagon Rules

Goal: Be the first to build a path of your colored stones connecting your two sides.

Movement: Place one stone per turn on any empty space.

The Trap: If you sandwich one opponent's stone between two of your stones in a straight line, the opponent's stone is removed from the board.

Note: You cannot trap your own stone by moving into a sandwich (Suicide moves are allowed but don't result in self-capture).
