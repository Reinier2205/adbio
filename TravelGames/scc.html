The Captain's Table

Roll 5 dice up to 3 times. You must first secure the Ship (6), then the Captain (5), then the Crew (4) — in that order.

Once all three are locked, the remaining dice are your Cargo. The player with the highest cargo score wins.

You may bank your cargo early after the first roll if you're happy with your score.
