✦ How to Use the AI Prompter

This tool helps you write structured prompts that get better results from AI assistants like Copilot or ChatGPT.

  • Pick a preset from the left to auto-fill the Context, Intent, and Format fields.
  • Paste your content into the Specific Information field — the email, report, or problem text.
  • Adjust any field to fine-tune the prompt.
  • Tap Build & Open to assemble the prompt, copy it, and open the AI in a new tab — then just paste.
  • Or tap Copy Only to copy without opening a browser tab.

The four fields (Context · Specific Information · Intent · Format) map to a proven prompt structure that gives AI models clear instructions and consistent output.
