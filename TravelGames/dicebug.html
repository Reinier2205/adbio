🐛 How to Play Dice Bug

Take turns rolling the die. When you get a part you can add, draw it on the canvas. Then tap Pass to hand the device to the other player. First to complete their bug wins!

Reference bug — aim for something like this:

① Large oval body
② Round head on top
③ 3 leg pairs (6 legs)
④ 2 eyes on head
⑤ 2 antennae from head
⑥ Tail at bottom
Draw freely — it's your bug! 🎨
  • You must roll Body (①) before any other part.
  • Head (②) is required before eyes or antennae.
  • After rolling, draw your part, then tap Pass.
  • If your roll gives nothing new, just tap Pass.
