ğŸ“œ Historical Context: A Golden Era

The foundation of the entire route is the Transvaal Gold Rush of the late 1800s.

  • Pilgrim's Rest: The entire town is a designated National Monument—a living museum that preserves the architecture and spirit of the gold-seeking era.
  • Famous Names: Many natural features were named by the prospectors. The area is also the setting for the classic South African novel, Jock of the Bushveld.
