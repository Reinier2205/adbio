How to Play: 3D Dice Roller

Roll two 3D dice with friends, track turns, and enjoy the casino feel.

Setup

  • Tap Add Player to add participants. Rename by tapping the pencil.

Gameplay

  • Tap Roll Dice on your turn to roll both dice.
  • Turns advance automatically to the next player.

Tips

  • Use Reset Game to clear players and start over.
