WhatsApp Business Setup Guidelines

Prerequisites for WhatsApp Business Setup

1. Business Phone Number

A dedicated business phone number (avoid personal numbers)
Once registered, the number cannot be changed without re-registering
Use a professional phone number, preferably linked to a business line

2. Business Name

Your official business name as seen by customers
Cannot be changed after registration (choose carefully!)
Keep it short, recognizable, and avoid unnecessary symbols or emojis

3. Business Logo

A high-quality square image representing your brand
No specific file size limit, but square format recommended for optimal display
Use a clean logo with a transparent background for a professional look

4. Business Description

A short summary of your business
Limited to 139 characters
Be clear, engaging, and precise (mention your services/products concisely)

5. Business Category

Select a category that best represents your business
Once set, modifying it requires account settings update
Pick the most relevant category to help customers find you

6. Business Address

Your physical address (if applicable)
Address visibility depends on business type
If you operate online, mention "Online Store" or relevant details

7. Operating Hours

Define your working hours for customer visibility
Customers may see outdated hours if not updated regularly
Keep them accurate and update based on seasonal changes

8. Website & Email

Your official website and business email for contact
Only one website link can be added to the profile
Use a professional domain rather than a generic email (e.g., info@yourbrand.com)

9. Product/Service Catalog

High-quality product images, descriptions, and prices
Up to 500 products/services can be listed
Keep product names short, descriptions clear, and images professional

10. Message Templates

Pre-written Greeting Messages, Away Messages, and Quick Replies
Character limits apply based on message type
Personalize templates to sound human and engaging instead of robotic

11. Labels for Chats

Organize chats using colored labels (e.g., New Customer, Pending Payment)
You can create up to 20 labels
Use labels efficiently to categorize customers and track progress
