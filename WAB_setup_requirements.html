Business Phone Number

Phone Number Requirements

Prerequisite

A dedicated business phone number that can receive SMS and calls for verification.

Restriction

Cannot use a landline number. Must be a mobile number that can receive SMS.

Best Practice

Use a dedicated business number separate from your personal WhatsApp to maintain professionalism.

Business Name

Business Name Guidelines

Prerequisite

Your official business name as registered or commonly known to customers.

Restriction

Cannot use generic terms or misleading names. Must be your actual business name.

Best Practice

Use a consistent business name across all platforms for better brand recognition.

Business Logo

Logo Specifications

Prerequisite

A clear, professional logo representing your business.

Restriction

Logo must be appropriate and not contain prohibited content. Maximum size: 5MB.

Best Practice

Use a high-quality, square logo with transparent background for best display.

Business Description

Description Guidelines

Prerequisite

A brief description of your business and services.

Restriction

Maximum 512 characters. No spam or misleading content.

Best Practice

Include key services, unique selling points, and business hours.

Business Category

Category Selection

Prerequisite

Select the most appropriate category for your business.

Restriction

Must choose from predefined categories. Cannot be changed frequently.

Best Practice

Choose the most specific category that matches your business type.

Business Address

Address Requirements

Prerequisite

Your business location or service area.

Restriction

Must be a valid address. Cannot use PO boxes for verification.

Best Practice

Include clear directions or landmarks for better customer location.

Operating Hours

Hours Guidelines

Prerequisite

Your business operating hours.

Restriction

Must be accurate and regularly updated.

Best Practice

Include special hours for holidays and peak seasons.

Website & Email

Contact Information

Prerequisite

Business website and email address.

Restriction

Email must be a valid business email address.

Best Practice

Use a professional email address matching your business domain.

Product/Service Catalog

Catalog Requirements

Prerequisite

List of products or services you offer.

Restriction

Products must comply with WhatsApp's commerce policies.

Best Practice

Include clear photos, prices, and descriptions for each item.

Message Templates

Template Guidelines

Prerequisite

Pre-approved message templates for common interactions.

Restriction

Templates must be approved by WhatsApp before use.

Best Practice

Create templates for order confirmations, shipping updates, and FAQs.

Labels for Chats

Labeling System

Prerequisite

System for organizing and categorizing customer chats.

Restriction

Labels must be appropriate and professional.

Best Practice

Create labels for different stages of customer interaction (New, In Progress, Completed).
