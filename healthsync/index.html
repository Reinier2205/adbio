Who We Are

Health Synchrony is an integrated health practice nestled in the heart of Irene Security Estate, Centurion. Led by Dr. van Dyk — a University of Pretoria graduate and holder of an Advanced Diploma in Aesthetics — our team combines deep clinical expertise with a genuine passion for holistic wellness.

We believe that looking and feeling your best should be accessible, evidence-based, and deeply personal. Whether you are exploring aesthetic treatments, seeking a medical weight-loss programme, or simply investing in your long-term wellbeing, we provide a calm, luxurious environment where every treatment plan is tailored to you.
