1. Personal Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information when you use our website or contact our practice:

  • Identity information: full name
  • Contact details: email address, phone number, postal address
  • Appointment-related information: reason for enquiry, preferred contact method, appointment history
  • Communication preferences: your consent to appointment and marketing communications
  • Technical data: browser type, approximate location (country/region), pages visited (collected via analytics, if enabled)

2. Why We Collect Your Information

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries and schedule appointments
  • To deliver and manage healthcare services
  • To send appointment reminders and practice communications (with your consent)
  • To send educational and marketing communications (only if you have explicitly consented)
  • To comply with our legal and professional obligations under South African law
  • To improve the quality and accessibility of our website and services

3. Your Rights

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: request the deletion of your personal information where we no longer have a lawful basis to retain it
  • Objection: object to the processing of your information for direct marketing purposes
  • Withdraw consent: withdraw your consent to marketing communications at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@healthsynchrony.co.za or by phone at +27 XX XXX XXXX.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Our staff are trained on data privacy obligations and access to patient information is restricted to authorised personnel only.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with trusted third parties (such as appointment scheduling platforms) only where necessary to deliver our services and only under appropriate data processing agreements.

6. Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes described above, or as required by law. Medical records are retained in accordance with the Health Professions Act.
