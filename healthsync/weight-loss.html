Your Weight Loss Journey

Our structured programme guides you from your first consultation to long-term maintenance. Each step is shaped by your individual health profile and goals.

  1. Initial Consultation Meet Dr. van Dyk for a comprehensive health assessment covering your medical history, lifestyle, and weight management goals. We take the time to understand you as a whole person before recommending any programme.
  2. Personalised Plan Receive a tailored weight management plan designed around your metabolism, health status, and daily routine. Your programme may include dietary guidance, lifestyle adjustments, and medical support where appropriate.
  3. Treatment Programme Begin your structured programme with the full support of Dr. van Dyk and the Health Synchrony team. All medical interventions are evidence-based and subject to ongoing clinical review throughout your journey.
  4. Progress Check-ins Regular follow-up consultations allow us to monitor your progress, adjust your plan as needed, and celebrate milestones along the way. Your wellbeing remains our priority at every stage.
  5. Maintenance & Lifestyle Transition into a long-term maintenance phase with tools and guidance to sustain your results and build lasting healthy habits. Our team remains available for continued support and encouragement.

Expert Medical Guidance You Can Trust
